About Our Church

The vision of the Anglican Church of Kenya (ACK) is a strengthened Anglican Church built on the foundation of the apostolic faith in Jesus Christ, equipping all God's people to face the challenges of the new millennium.

Its mission is to bring all people into a living relationship with God through Jesus Christ—through preaching, teaching, healing, social transformation—and enabling believers to grow in faith and live life in its fullness.

The ACK operates under an episcopal system and is comprised of 41 dioceses, each led by a bishop. The church is currently headed by Archbishop Jackson Ole Sapit.

ACK Governance Structure
  • Dioceses – led by Bishops
  • Archdeaconries – led by Archdeacons
  • Deaneries – led by Rural Deans
  • Parishes – led by Vicars
  • Local Church Committees (LCCs)
  • Parish Church Councils (PCCs)
Administrative Functions – Thika Diocese

The Diocese operates under the principle of being Episcopally led and Synodically governed. Key responsibilities are executed by:

Standing Committee of Synod:
  • Acts on matters from Provincial Synod, Diocesan Synod, and other church bodies
  • Receives and processes reports from diocesan boards
  • Fills diocesan vacancies between synods
Board of Finance:
  • Advises on and manages diocesan financial matters
  • Prepares and audits diocesan and parochial accounts
  • Promotes Christian stewardship and financial transparency
  • Coordinates fundraising and budgeting activities

Community Outreach Ministry

Our Community Outreach Ministry is dedicated to serving those in need within our local community and beyond. We believe in demonstrating God's love through practical acts of service and compassion.

Our initiatives include:

  • Food Distribution Programs: Providing meals and groceries to vulnerable families.
  • Medical Camps: Offering free health screenings and basic medical care.
  • Education Support: Assisting children and youth with school supplies and mentorship.
  • Environmental Clean-ups: Participating in local clean-up drives to care for creation.
  • Visitations: Visiting the sick, elderly, and incarcerated to offer comfort and support.

We collaborate with local organizations and volunteers to maximize our impact and reach more individuals. Join us in making a tangible difference in the lives of others!

How You Can Get Involved
  • Volunteer: Dedicate your time and skills to our various programs.
  • Donate: Contribute financially or provide in-kind donations (food, clothes, medical supplies).
  • Pray: Support our ministry through prayer for guidance and provision.
