About Our Church

ACK St. Stephen Church Ngoingwa is a vibrant Anglican Christian community committed to sharing the love of Christ, supporting families, nurturing spiritual growth, and serving the people of Ngoingwa and beyond. We are part of the Anglican Church of Kenya and welcome everyone to join us in worship, fellowship, and service.
